Dr. Satish Baburao Shelke

Associate Professor of Physics

Dr. Satish Shelke is the son of an eminent educationalist Late Baburao Shelke Guruji, who guided him to become a well talented, brave, ethical and a man of character. He learnt the lessons of discipline, dedication and hard work right from his childhood days.

He has realized the fact that 'The cosmic world is a stage, we all are puppets to perform as per the assigned characters'.

Journey

In 2007, he got selected for the post of an Assistant Professor. He has teaching experience of Under graduate, post graduate and research centre. He has successfully guided Ph. D. scholars and published a number of research papers in national and international journals.

He has rendered his services as a college co-ordinator in UGC, AISHE, MIS, NAAC & NIRF. He made vital contribution to achieve the college targets for the coordination and determination of standards in Institutions of Higher Education, Quality Teaching and Research.

He is also associated with several Committees constituted by University, Curriculum Development Committees, Affiliation Committee, Observer for PET-2017 and so on. He was uncontested for Sectional Committee Member of Indian Science Congress in 2017-18.

Blessed with natural skills on extempore subject matters, he delivered lectures in Universities and Colleges at National and International Seminars, Conferences and Workshops. He has chaired and organized a number of National Seminars, Conferences and Workshops.

He is life member of various professional bodies like Materials Research Society of India, Indian association of Physics Teachers, Indian Science Congress & Indian Physics Association. He is an Associate Editor, Guest Editorial and Reviewer of Bio-info Publications and also Member of Guest Editorial Board of Bio-info Publications.

He was honoured by various awards by Govt of India and various organisations. He is also honoured by District Youth Award, Govt of India by Nehru Yuva Centre, Marathwada Youth Award and Marathwada Ratna Award for social work.

He was selected for World Peace Conference, youth leader camp for secretary of student council by Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar University, Aurangabad. He was also selected for ‘National Integration Camp’ at Amravati, M.S. He has also represented in the meetings of various regulatory bodies and Institutions of higher education along with other academicians.

He has visited most of the leading universities, colleges of the country in various capacities and closely observed the teaching learning process, research, quality assurance, educational systems, infrastructure, use of technology, governance and other dimensions of higher education.

Such practical approach made his vision deep and penetrative about the dominating factors of discrimination prevalent at social, economical, regional and gender levels of Indian society.
